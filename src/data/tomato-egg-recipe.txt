Ingredients:\n
- tomatoes\n
- eggs\n
- ginger\n
- scallion\n
- cheese slice (optional)\n
- soy sauce\n
- salt\n
\n
Instructions:\n
1. Chop tomatoes into chunks\n
2. Scramble eggs, add salt\n
3. Heat ginger in oil in pan\n
4. Add tomatoes, salt, soy sauce, cook until softened\n
5. Add scrambled eggs and scallion, cook until eggs are set\n
6. Add cheese slice on top if desired.
