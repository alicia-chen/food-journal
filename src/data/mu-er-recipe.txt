Ingredients: 
- dried wood ear mushrooms (木耳)
- ground turkey or pork
- ginger
- scallions
- soy sauce
- black vinegar
- shaoxing wine (optional)
- white pepper (optional)

Steps: 
1. Soak dried wood ears in water for at least 20 minutes, then wash and drain
2. Heat oil in pan, add ginger
3. Cook ground meat with soy sauce, salt, white pepper
4. Add wood ears, black vinegar, shaoxing wine
5. Add scallions
